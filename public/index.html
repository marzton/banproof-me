// Proof of Agency

An audit layer for the age of autonomous AI.

Proof of Agency is a protocol that creates an immutable, human-readable record of every AI action chain. Not just logs — structured provenance that can be inspected, challenged, and verified by anyone with access.

⬡

Action provenance

Every AI action is tied to a chain: which model, which prompt, which data, which human authorization event. Nothing anonymous.

⬡

Human-in-the-loop gates

Consequential actions pause for explicit human approval before proceeding. The gate is the record — bypass attempts are themselves logged.

⬡

Immutable audit trail

Records write to D1 via Cloudflare Workers and cannot be altered after the fact. Every record is timestamped, hashed, and verifiable.

⬡

Cross-system visibility

Works across the Gold Shore Labs ecosystem and any system that integrates the PoA SDK. One record format, any AI provider.

⬡

Sentiment scoring

Optional AI-powered analysis of each action's intent and output, scored and stored alongside the PoA record for downstream querying.

⬡

DePIN infrastructure

Built on decentralized-friendly principles. Records are portable, exportable, and not locked to a single vendor's infrastructure.

// Why it matters now

AI is everywhere. Accountability is not.

As AI agents take on real-world tasks — sending emails, executing trades, generating content, managing systems — the question stops being "can it do this?" and becomes "did it do this, and should it have?"

Banproof is the infrastructure layer that answers that question. Built by Gold Shore Labs and designed to grow as AI becomes a standard operating layer for every business.
